About Cloud Health

Cloud Health is a web platform for health self-management.
We provide an open source and free platform for third-party organizations to develop chronic disease management applications.
With one unified platform, patients with multiple chronic diseases or unique conditions can coordinate their own care with the help of their caregivers.
Our platform provides user and caregiver registration and messaging, and a modern modular web framework for developers.

We vow to provide this platform freely and openly for public or private use and encourage the sharing of health self-management modules.
